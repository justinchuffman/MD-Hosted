# One Pot Creamy Tomato Orzo

## Ingredients

- 1 cup of dry orzo
- 2 cups of spinach
- 1 lb of ground beef
- 1/2 medium-sized onion, diced
- 1 tsp olive oil
- dash red pepper flakes
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 4-6 minced garlic cloves
- 15 oz tomato sauce
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup of beef broth
- 3/4 cup of heavy cream
- fresh grated parm
- 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Dice your onion.
2. Get a pot and set the temperature to medium heat.
3. Pour in 1 tsp of olive oil.
4. Once your pan is hot enough, add your ground beef and brown it.
5. Once your beef is browned, leave it in the pan but remove SOME of the fat. Not all of it. Leave about 1-2 tbsp of beef fat.
6. Add your onion and sauté for 5 minutes.
7. Don't forget to season with salt and pepper AS YOU GO. Not just once.
8. Once your onions are see-thru and you can smell them cooking, add a tsp of red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning and 4-6 cloves of garlic.
9. Once you can smell the garlic, add in your dried orzo.
10. Hit it with 2 tbsp of tomato paste and mix well.
11. Let everything toast together for about 2 minutes or until you can smell the tomato paste cooking and the orzo toasting.
12. Deglaze with 1 cup of beef broth and then add your tomato sauce.
13. Deglaze is just a fancy word for get the stuck brown bits off the bottom of the pot.
14. Add your heavy cream, Worcestershire and balsamic and mix the shit well.
15. Bring everything to a gentle simmer and allow it to thicken up.
16. Don't simmer it too hot or the heavy cream can separate and the orzo will stick.
17. After about 10 minutes, turn the heat off and grate in 1/2 cup of fresh parm and add 2 cups of fresh spinach.
18. Remove it from the burner and allow the spinach to wilt.
